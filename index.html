About me

My name is Sarah Khalaf. I'm a 23 years old young lady. I was born and raised in Al-Salt, a calm ancient city full of green areas. It's located in west-central Jordan. I've grown up in small lovely supportive family. I went to Al-Maymounah high school and then I have been graduated from Applied English department in Al-Ahliyya Amman University.

When I was younger, My family encouraged me to try a lot of hobbies and activities in order to better understand myself and find my passion. I found myself interested mostly in reading, swimming and I have recently been introduced to Yoga which I practice now so often.

When I went to school I faced many problems and I was frustrated by the educational system, both in school and university. Now I work in that same educational system as an English teacher. That's why I decided to exert much efforts to apply my vision on that system to improve it and help the current students to avoid having the same problems that I had. I'm working on providing them with a better form of both enjoyable and effective training to my students.

I still love to spend my time in reading, practicing yoga, meeting different people from different backgrounds and cultures - as I believe in the importance and beauty of diversity - and I also like to travel to different places and enjoy being in natural places in my vacations.
